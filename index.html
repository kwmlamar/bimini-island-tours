Have questions before you book?

We've Got Answers

How do I book a tour?

Fill out our booking form on the website or send us a message on WhatsApp, Facebook, or Instagram at @Biminiislandtours. We'll confirm your tour date and details directly.

What tours do you offer?

We offer three main experiences: the North Bimini Heritage Tour (focusing on history, culture, and local food), the South Bimini: Ponce de León Tour (exploring the Fountain of Youth and Biological Field Station), and the Combination Tour, a comprehensive four-hour journey covering both North and South Bimini.

How long are the tours?

Our North and South Bimini tours are approximately 2 hours each. The full Combination Tour is around 4 hours.

How much does it cost?

Our North Bimini tour starts at $110, the South Bimini tour starts at $150, and the full Combination Tour starts at $199. Prices may vary slightly depending on your group size.

Where do we meet?

Once you disembark from your cruise ship, take the complimentary tram to the Casino stop at Resorts World Bimini. Your guide Max will be there to welcome you.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing comfortable walking shoes, reef-safe sunscreen, a camera or smartphone for photos, a hat, sunglasses, and some extra cash for souvenirs, gratuities, or the famous Bimini bread!

Is lunch or food included?

Depending on the tour, you'll have the opportunity to taste fresh conch and local bread. You can also opt for our Fish Fry lunch option during the booking intake process.

Can children join the tours?

Yes! Our tours are family-friendly and children of all ages are welcome. Please let us know if you are bringing small children so we can ensure the best experience for your family.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a full refund for cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance. For cancellations made within 24 hours, please contact us directly.

What if the weather is bad?

Tours operate rain or shine, but safety is our priority. If we must cancel due to severe weather or unsafe marine conditions, we will offer to reschedule or provide a full refund.

How do I pay?

We do not accept cash. Once your tour is confirmed, we will send you a secure payment link to complete your booking.

How can I contact you?

Reach us via WhatsApp or iMessage at 242-473-0233 or 242-442-2629, or message us on Facebook and Instagram at @Biminiislandtours.

Where to Find Us

Your Meeting Point

Once you disembark, take the complimentary tram to the Casino stop at Resorts World Bimini — it's the last stop, so just stay on until the end. The tram is free and runs continuously from the pier. Your guide will be waiting for you there.

While there's a bridge connecting the pier to town, walking isn't practical — the heat makes it uncomfortable and eats into your time on island. The tram is the way to go.
