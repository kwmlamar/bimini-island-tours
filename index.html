The Bimini Island Tours Story

Where Real Island Life Begins

Bimini is more than a destination — it's a feeling. Crystal-clear water, warm people, and an island pace that slows everything down to what matters. But most visitors only see the resort version. We want to show you the real thing.

Max is a native Biminite with deep roots across every corner of the island. He built Bimini Island Tours on one simple belief: the best way to experience a place is through the eyes of someone who loves it. Every tour is personal, unhurried, and built around you.
