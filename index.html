Your Adventure, Our Unique Expertise

We're a family-owned Biminite Bahamian business with roots woven deep into island life. Every experience we offer is shaped by the warmth, authenticity, and genuine hospitality that defines Bimini — and the people who call it home.

Culture & Heritage

The Dolphin House Museum, the Craft Center, the legendary Bimini Bread — living culture you can see, touch, and taste.

Historic Footsteps

Walk paths once traveled by Martin Luther King Jr., Ernest Hemingway, and Ponce de Leon across Bimini's storied landscape.

Nature & Ecology

Discover Bimini's vital mangrove ecosystems and the groundbreaking research at the Bimini Biological Shark Lab.

Landmark Experiences

Leave footprints on Radio Beach, trace the Silence of the Lambs film route, and find corners most visitors never reach.

Our tours are intentionally uncrowded — private or guided — so every moment feels personal, unhurried, and authentically Bahamian.

A Message from Max

"Bimini is my home, and I want it to feel like yours. When you book with us, you're not just getting a tour — you're getting a friend who genuinely wants to show you the real island. I've spent my whole life here, and nothing gives me more joy than sharing the beauty of Bimini with visitors from all over the world. The beaches, the history, the people — there's nowhere quite like it, and I can't wait to show you why."
