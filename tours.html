Island Heritage Tour

Bimini is one of the oldest settled islands in the Bahamas, and Max knows every corner of its history. Walk through local neighborhoods, visit iconic landmarks, and hear stories passed down through generations of islanders.

  • Historic landmarks and sites

Snorkeling & Sea Life

Bimini sits on the edge of the Gulf Stream, making it one of the best snorkeling destinations in the Bahamas. Explore vibrant reefs, encounter tropical marine life, and swim in some of the clearest water you will ever see.

  • Pristine reef snorkeling spots
  • Tropical fish, rays, and sea turtles
  • Crystal-clear Bahamian water
  • Max knows the best hidden swimming spots
  • Equipment guidance available

Blue Hole Adventure

Bimini's natural blue holes are among the most spectacular geological features in the Bahamas. These mystical underwater formations attract divers and explorers from around the world — and Max knows exactly where to find the best ones.

  • Bimini's legendary natural blue holes
  • Scenic boat ride through the islands
  • Underwater exploration opportunities
  • Stunning photography spots
  • Local geological and natural history
