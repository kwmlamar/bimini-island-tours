Core Tour Experiences
4 Hours

Combination Tour — Full Bimini Experience

Get ready to discover the very best of Bimini in one unforgettable adventure that blends history, culture, natural beauty, island flavors, and breathtaking scenery into a seamless guided experience across both North and South Bimini.

Read More

Your journey begins in colorful Alice Town, where the vibrant spirit of island life comes alive through charming streets, local culture, and authentic Bahamian hospitality. Cruise past stunning white-sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters while uncovering the stories, legends, and rich heritage that make Bimini unlike anywhere else in the world.

Along the way, guests may experience iconic highlights such as the famous Dolphin House Museum, Heroes Park, the Bimini Craft Centre, scenic marinas lined with luxury yachts, and panoramic coastal views stretching across the Atlantic. Hear fascinating stories surrounding Ernest Hemingway’s connection to Bimini and the mystery of the legendary Bimini Road, often linked to the Lost City of Atlantis.

The adventure continues with a scenic ferry ride to South Bimini, where guests will experience a quieter and more untouched side of the island. Discover breathtaking shorelines, Shell Beach, the legendary Fountain of Youth area associated with Ponce de León, and the world-renowned Bimini Biological Field Station, known globally for marine research and shark conservation.

Throughout the experience, guests enjoy authentic island storytelling, local food sampling including conch and traditional Bimini bread, scenic photo opportunities, and the warm hospitality that defines the Bahamian experience.

From hidden gems and cultural landmarks to unforgettable ocean views and island traditions, every moment of the Combination Tour is designed to immerse guests in the true essence of Bimini — all in one extraordinary experience.

Bimini Sit-Low Sightseeing

Get ready to experience Bimini like never before—without ever leaving your seat. This exhilarating, seamlessly guided tour sweeps you across the island’s most iconic sights, offering a vibrant snapshot of its charm, history, and natural beauty in one unforgettable journey.

Glide past powdery white beaches kissed by turquoise waters, where the Atlantic sparkles in every shade of blue imaginable. Soak in panoramic views of the legendary Bimini Road, steeped in mystery and intrigue, and catch glimpses of the storied shoreline once frequented by Ernest Hemingway himself.
